##### Introduction

**Welcome to {{ lab_name }}** — a dedicated bioinformatics environment for eDNA practitioners across Australia, New Zealand and the Pacific, built on the Galaxy {{ site_name }} platform by Australian BioCommons and the Southern eDNA Society.

{{ lab_name }} brings together the tools you need for every stage of eDNA metabarcoding analysis — from raw data quality control and sequence manipulation, through taxonomic assignment and community-level inference, to visualisation and export — all accessible through an intuitive graphical interface. No command-line expertise required.

The Galaxy platform adds further power: tools can be linked into end-to-end workflows that are reproducible, shareable, and reusable across projects and teams. Whether you work with short or long read data, in freshwater, marine or terrestrial systems, or across pure and applied research, {{ lab_name }} is designed to support rigorous, efficient analysis.

Our toolshed is actively maintained and developed in response to community needs. If you have feedback, encounter a gap, or want to suggest an addition, we'd love to hear from you.
